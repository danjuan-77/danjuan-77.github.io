I am jointly supervised by Prof. [Xie Chen](https://chenxie95.github.io/) from the [X-LANCE Lab](https://x-lance.sjtu.edu.cn/) and Dr. [Zilong Zheng](https://zilongzheng.github.io/) from the [Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI)](https://www.bigai.ai/), NLCo Group.
